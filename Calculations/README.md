🤖 4 Axis Robot Calculations

This page contains engineering calculations and design validation work for my 4-Axis Robot Arm project.
It documents the reasoning behind design decisions, component sizing, and load analysis used.

! Things may be imperfect at the start, but they are continuously improved through iteration and validation !


⚙️ What’s Included

This page will include calculations for:

- Joint Load Analysis
- [Motor + Gearbox Selection & Sizing]([url](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bGajeUhM4DlYe-yPef_99O2zEstQHPLJXcdyXvrxyio/edit?usp=sharing))
- Bearing load estimation
- Belt-driven wrist Calculation
- Joint Shaft sizing


📊 Key Engineering Assumptions

- Payload considered: 2 kg
- Gravity: 9.81 m/s²
- Safety factor: 1.5x - 2× depending on motion
- Design goal: smooth motion with minimal backlash and deflection
